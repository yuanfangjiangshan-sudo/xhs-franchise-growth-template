## Source Selection Rule

For daily generation:

- use 1 primary source tied to the topic
- use 1 supporting source for proof or brand context
- avoid loading unrelated source materials

## Red Lines

Never use:

- customer private data
- contracts
- unpublished financials
- exact revenue claims
- personal contact information
- confidential screenshots
