# Benchmark Research

Use benchmark research to learn structure, not to copy content.

## Minimum Research

When available, inspect at least:

- 3 recent same-topic Xiaohongshu notes
- 1 account with a similar audience
- 1 high-engagement title or cover pattern

If live search is unavailable, ask the user to provide examples or screenshots.

## What To Extract

For each benchmark, summarize:

- topic angle
- title pattern
- opening hook
- page structure
- comment trigger
- why it may fit or not fit the user's account

## Borrowing Rules

Allowed:

- structural patterns
- reader-pain framing
- title logic
- page sequencing
- comment-trigger mechanics

Not allowed:

- copying text
- copying images
- copying personal identity
- copying proprietary claims
- inventing performance data

## Useful Pattern Types

- mistake correction: "很多人以为 X，其实问题在 Y"
- operator judgment: "真正拉开差距的不是 X，而是 Y"
- checklist: "做 X 前先看这 5 点"
- case reflection: "一个小店/校区/团队的变化说明了什么"
- risk warning: "别急着做 X，先确认 Y"
